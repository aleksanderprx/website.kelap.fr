FREQUENTLY

ASKED

How much does a professional website cost?

The price varies depending on the project's complexity: number of pages, specific features, integrations, custom design… Every website is unique, which is why we provide a personalized quote after understanding your needs. Contact us to get yours.

Are there any hidden fees after delivery?

None whatsoever. Once your website is delivered, it's yours. No hidden fees, no surprise costs. The agreed price is the final price.

How long does it take to build a website?

It depends on the scope. For a one-page showcase website, expect around 10 days. A more complex site with multiple pages and features will naturally take longer. We set a clear timeline together from the start.

Can I request changes after the site goes live?

Of course. Simply reach out to us and we'll make the updates you need. The cost will depend on the nature and scope of the changes requested.

What does the process look like from start to finish?

It's simple and transparent. First, a call to understand your business, target audience and goals. Then, we create a visual mockup for your approval. Once validated, we develop the site entirely from scratch. You receive the final result ready to go live.

Do I need to provide the texts and images?

It's not mandatory, but strongly recommended. Your own images and texts bring an authenticity that nothing can replace and strengthen your brand identity on the site.

Will I be able to edit my website content myself?

It depends on the type of changes. We can set up an admin system so you're autonomous for routine updates. It's an additional option tailored to your needs.

Will my site rank well on Google?

Yes. We integrate SEO from the design phase. Together, we define the strategic keywords you want to rank for, and we optimize every page accordingly.

Will the site be mobile and tablet friendly?

Absolutely. All our sites are built with responsive design and adapt perfectly to every screen: desktop, tablet and mobile.

Why hire an agency instead of using Wix or WordPress?

A website built with a free tool is one among millions. We build sites that convert. Our expertise brings real value to your project: a unique design, a conversion strategy, and a site that truly works for your business.
